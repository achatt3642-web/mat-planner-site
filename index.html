Workflow

From DICOM or NIfTI through export.

  1. Upload knee MRI: DICOM series folder or NIfTI volume.
  2. Generate femur and tibia bone masks.
  3. Run 3D CNN meniscus sizing (medial and lateral width and length).
  4. Estimate horn coordinates and visualize the predicted graft.
  5. Use the graft tab for Exact, Overlay, and Edited views; adjust AP, ML, and SI.
  6. Review tibial contact surface area and tunnel or trough planning targets.
  7. Export a PDF report and JSON summary.

Local launch

After you unzip the download.

Quick start

macOS: double-click launch_mac.command
Windows: double-click launch_windows.bat
